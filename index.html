      Avengers: Endgame is a 2019 American superhero film based on Marvel Comics' Avengers, produced by Marvel Studios, and published by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. The film is the last Avengers series to follow the 2012 film Avengers, the 2015 film Avengers: Age of Ultron, and the 2018 film Infinity War, and the 22nd installment of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). Directed by Anthony Russo and Joruso, the screenplay was co-written by Christopher Marcus and Stephen McFeely, and features more actors and ensemble casts than previous MCU films. In the film, surviving members of the Avengers attempt to undo the destruction Thanos caused in Infinity War.
